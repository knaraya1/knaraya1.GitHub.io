Website Evaluations

Yale University School of Art

This is a website for the Yale University that offers undergraduate-level art courses.

  • C: The page has strong contrast between each of its sections. However, the headings' low contrast with the dark background makes some of them hard to read.
  • R: Some of the sections of the page are repeated, while other sections are only displayed once and stand out too much.
  • A: The page features some alignment on the left, but other sections are scattered throughout the page, especially near the center.
  • P: Only some related sections are together, while others which are different are spread or also close together

Amazon

This website is for shopping and ordering online.

  • C: The colors of the text and the background of the website are clearly different and make all text easy to read.
  • R: The page repeats similar sections in a grid like layout that make navigating easier.
  • A: The alignment of the grid layout is neat, as well as the content of the footer.
  • P: All the different sections in the grid layout are close together while the nav bar is separated with a banner.
